General Overview of Ruby

Ruby is a dynamically-typed, interpreted programming language known for its simplicity, readability, and flexibility. It is often used for web development, scripting, and building applications.

Ruby has a strong emphasis on code readability and developer productivity, with a focus on simplicity and minimalism. It is also known for its strong support for object-oriented programming and its powerful metaprogramming capabilities.
