General Overview of Golang

Go (also known as Golang) is a programming language developed by Google in 2009. It is a statically-typed, compiled language designed to be simple, efficient, and scalable. Go is known for its concurrency support.

It also has a strong emphasis on reliability and security, making it well-suited for building large-scale, distributed systems. Go is often used for building web servers, microservices, and other types of networked applications.
